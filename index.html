Our Equipment

"At our premier hockey academy, we pride ourselves on providing an extensive array of top-notch equipment to ensure a comprehensive and enriching experience for our players. From cutting-edge playing gear to state-of-the-art training aids, our academy is equipped with everything needed to foster skill development, safety, and a genuine love for the game. Our players benefit from a diverse selection of high-quality hockey sticks, precision-engineered goalie equipment, and regulation-size goals. The protective gear we provide, including certified helmets, custom jerseys, and top-of-the-line skating equipment, ensures that each athlete is fully equipped for both practice and game days. Training at our academy goes beyond the basics. We offer a variety of training aids such as shooting targets, agility cones, and stickhandling balls to enhance skill sets. Our goalie training equipment, including rebounders and shooter tutors, is designed to refine techniques and improve overall performance between the pipes. With a commitment to excellence, our academy is not just about the game; it's about creating a professional and inspiring environment. Our facility is complete with boards and glass for a regulation-sized rink, a scoreboard to track progress, and a well-organized storage system for efficient access to equipment. In addition to prioritizing player needs, we've invested in coach and referee essentials, including coaching boards, whistles, and hydration stations. This ensures that our coaching staff is equipped to lead and inspire while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism. At Hockey's, we don't just have equipment; we have a comprehensive arsenal designed to elevate the hockey experience for every player. Join us in a space where passion meets preparation, and the love for the game is nurtured with the best equipment available."
